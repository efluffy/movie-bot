  Moviebot Help
-----------------
This bot will maintain a list of movies that people add along with votes, and on each movie night the bot will select a random movie from the top 5 highest voted on the list to watch!

Commands:

!add - For adding a movie to the list. 
	usage: "!add <movie title>"

!rem - Allows you to remove a movie from the list.
	usage: "!rem <movie id>"
	
!vote - Allows voting on a specific movie.
	usage: "!vote <movie id>"

!list - Returns a list of currently saved movies, votes, and IDs.
	usage: "!list"
